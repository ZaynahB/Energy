Solar Roofs

Tuesday, November 19, 2019

19 Nov.

By: Zaynah Bhanji
Should solar panel roofs be mandated?

Tesla's solar roof is essentially a roofing system that looks similar to traditional roofs but has solar cells embedded within it. Tesla just released their V3 Solar Roof a few weeks ago and it is targeted at people who need a new roof. This brings up the question of if solar panels should be mandated or not. In my opinion, there are multiple things we need to consider when thinking about this question.

I think the first thing we need to consider is the technology itself. While solar panels themselves are very efficient, current battery technology is not enough to store all of the energy that solar panels are able to harness. We need a huge innovation in lithium-ion batteries or we need to make batteries in a new type of way for a cheaper cost so that we are able to store greater amounts of solar energy.

Another downside of this is that the initial upfront cost of installing solar panels is very high, and even though it does pay back after a few years, families with lower incomes may not be able to afford installing solar panels or solar roofs on their houses.

However, there are many upsides to solar panels. Firstly, solar energy is a renewable energy source, meaning that it will never run out. Solar energy is also very clean compared to other energy sources such as oil or coal. The transportation, manufacturing, and installation of solar panels does emit greenhouse gases, however it is almost nothing compared to the amount of pollution caused by fossil fuels.

I believe that currently it does not make sense to make solar panels mandatory because of the fact that in the next year or two, I believe the cost will go down immensely and the technology will get a lot better. With new innovations such as quantum dot solar cells and solar paint, we will be able to greatly enhance this technology and make it even better. For this reason, I believe we should wait for at least one or two years and then make the use of solar energy mandatory.
